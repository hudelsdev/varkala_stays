Moon Waves Beach Resort

Moon Waves Beach Resort

Varkala Beach, Varkala | 2 minutes walk to Varkala Beach



About

Located in Varkala, a 3-minute walk from Varkala Beach, Moon Waves Beach Resort provides accommodations with a terrace, free private parking, and a restaurant.
The property is around 46.7 km from Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, 46.7 km from Napier Museum, and a 16-minute walk from Janardhana swamy Temple.
The property has a 24-hour front desk, airport transportation, room service, and free WIFI. At the resort, every room has a desk. With a private bathroom equipped with a shower and free toiletries,
certain Moon Waves Beach Resort units also provide guests with a patio.
